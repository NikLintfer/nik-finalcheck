Longboards

Longboards are nice boards for beginner surfers as they are stable and very bouyant. They are also very fast so it is easy to catch waves with them. Longboards are often used on smaller waves.

Shortboards

Shortboards are harder to use than longboards and are gnerally used by pro surfers. With shortboards you can take sharper turns and do cool tricks.

Gun Boards

Gun boards have the same shape as short boards but are much longer and heavier. These boards are used for big wave surfing
