Pipeline

Pipeline is one of the most famous surf spots in the world, located on the north shore of Ohau Hawaii. The wave is considered extremly dangerous and should only be surfed by pros.

Teahupoo

Teahupoo is located in the french polonesian islands and is considerd the worlds most perfect and beautiful wabve. The wave crashes on extremly shallow reef and should therefore only be suref by pros.

Uluwatu

This left hand break is located in Bali Indonesia and is a very consistant wave that breaks all year around. This wave can be ridden by intermediates on smaller days and pros on bigger days.
